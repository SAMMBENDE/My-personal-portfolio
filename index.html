How I got into

While I enjoyed my previous role as a tech vendor for five years, the last year was so stressful that it prompted me to consider other career options. I gave myself a moment of break and looked into IT and its career possibilities.
With a willingness to reach the goals I set for myself, I tapped inspiration from those with experience in the field who helped open my eyes to this side of the decor.
During the COVID-19 pandemic, I adapted my schedule for a remote learning environment and earned two verifiable IT badges with IBM. As curiosity gained control, I started learning web development with the help of online resources myself, and the more I delved in, the more I realized there was so much to learn. I then took some intensive training sessions on web and mobile web development.
As a full-stack javascript developer, I always consider adaptive and responsive layouts when building UIs for optimal user experience.
At the moment I'm very excited about setting up a digital marketing agency, DigicorePro, with the hope of acquiring various projects and building a solid client base.
