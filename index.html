How I got into the world of

Ex multimedia full-time vendor and a part-time DJ on weekends, I felt the need to promote my mixtapes and also build an audience on the web. The idea of having a website started hovering in my mind. As curiosity gained control, I did some research with online resources on how to create a website. From that moment on, I developed a passion for programming. It seemed magical, and the more I delved into it, the more I realized that I was the magician orchestrating the results and that there was so much more to learn.
