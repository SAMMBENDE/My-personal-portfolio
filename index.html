About me

How I got into

My name is Sampson. I’m a full-stack developer, and I specialize in Javascript & React apps that work across all platforms and browsers. I take very seriously adaptive and responsive layouts when building UIs for optimal user experience.
As a professional DJ, I felt the need to promote my mixtapes and also build an audience on the web. The idea of having a website started hovering in my mind. As curiosity gained control, I did some research with online resources on how to create a website. From that moment on, I developed a passion for programming. It seemed magical, and the more I delved into it, the more I realized that I was the magician orchestrating the results and that there was so much more to learn. So I continued learning on my own before taking an intensive training program on web development. Right now, I’m excited about putting my skills into practice with a first professional experience as an intern and working towards becoming a React senior.
